ABOUT US

Who What Wear is an international fashion company known for its content sites—WhoWhatWear.com and WhoWhatWear.co.uk—and its affordable, size-inclusive, and trend-forward line of clothing and accessories.

Our vision is to change the way women feel about fashion and beauty by continuing to champion the idea that style is inclusive and attainable by all.

 
 

OUR FOUNDERS

Katherine Power and Hillary Kerr founded Who What Wear in 2006 with the ambitious idea to reimagine how women consume content and shop for products online. Katherine now serves as CEO and Hillary as chief content officer.
